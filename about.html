About Rotaract Club of Mumbai Ghatkopar

We are a dynamic community of passionate youth leaders committed to service, leadership, and fellowship. RCMG strives to make a positive difference through impactful projects, creative initiatives, and meaningful collaborations. Here's a glimpse of what defines us:

Who We Are

The Rotaract Club of Mumbai Ghatkopar is a vibrant platform for young individuals to explore leadership, drive change, and contribute to their communities. Our club comprises enthusiastic professionals and students who collaborate on service projects, professional development, and social initiatives, under the guidance of Rotary International.

Our Vision

To empower youth to become responsible global citizens and compassionate leaders, inspiring change through action, innovation, and inclusion. Our vision is to nurture a culture where service, empathy, and collaboration go hand-in-hand, making our world a better place.

Our Mission

To build a community of young change-makers dedicated to self-development, professional growth, and social impact. We aim to create meaningful avenues for service, foster leadership through responsibility, and bridge communities with purpose and passion.
